Field Guide

Understanding the BrainMap AI parameters and lead scoring logic.

Scoring Algorithm

+50 No Website

Highest priority for website development or digital presence services.

+20 Missing Email

Opportunity for lead generation and outreach automated systems.

+20 Low Rating

Applied when Google Maps rating is below 4.0 (Need for Reputation Mgmt).

+30 Missing Socials

Combined points if Instagram (+15) or Facebook (+15) are not found.

+20 Professional Gaps

Combined points for missing LinkedIn (+10) or X.com/Twitter (+10).

100 Premium Override

Automated fixed score for "Healthy" businesses with full presence but high growth potential.

Excel Parameter Definitions

Business Name

Official name registered on Google Maps.

Type

The primary business category or niche scraped (e.g., HVAC, Dentist).

Scoring

The calculated lead priority score (0-150) based on digital gaps.

Rating

Average star rating from Google Maps reviews.

Phone Number

Direct contact number extracted from listing or website.

Website

Raw URL of the business homepage.

Visit Website

Clickable hyperlink for quickly viewing the lead's current site.

Email

Verified emails scraped from the website contact/about pages.

Social Links (FB, IG, LI, X)

Direct links to official business profiles on major platforms.

Address & Map

Complete physical address and a direct link to the listing on Maps.
